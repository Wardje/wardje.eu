Software

  • Antennapod. My main consumption of podcasts is while I am doing other things. To easily listen to podcasts in such a situation, you need an app on your phone. I want something open source as well as avoiding reliance on big names. Availability on F-droid is also always a big plus. Antennapod checks the boxes.
  • castero. On a desktop I usually just want something to easily download episodes of a podcast for backup. I have not used castero much yet, but so far it seems to fit the bill. I do not need much, so a TUI client that quickly starts up is pretty nice.
